Zambia Mobile Money Payment Instructions

Airtel Zambia (Airtel Money)

  1. Dial *155# on your Airtel line
  2. Select "Send Money" from the menu
  3. Enter payment number: 0977123456
  4. Account Name: CLASSIC MAXIMIZE LTD
  5. Enter the amount you want to deposit (in ZMW)
  6. Enter your Airtel Money PIN to confirm
  7. Confirm the transaction to complete payment
  8. Save the transaction reference number for verification
Make sure you have sufficient balance in your Airtel Money account before proceeding with the payment. Airtel Money charges may apply.

MTN Zambia (MTN Mobile Money)

  1. Dial *155# on your MTN line
  2. Select "Send Money" from the menu
  3. Enter payment number: 0965987654
  4. Account Name: CLASSIC MAXIMIZE COMPANY
  5. Enter the amount you want to deposit (in ZMW)
  6. Enter your Mobile Money PIN to confirm
  7. Confirm the transaction to complete payment
  8. Save the transaction reference number for verification
Make sure you have sufficient balance in your MTN Mobile Money account before proceeding with the payment. MTN charges may apply.
